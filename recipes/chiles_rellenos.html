Chiles Rellenos

Description

Chiles stuffed with cheese michoacan style

Ingredients

  • Two squash
  • 4 eggs
  • Fresh chees (Queso fresco)
  • 1/3 of an onion
  • 5 tomatoes
  • 5 chilies to stuff
  • 2/3 cups of flour

Steps

  1. Brown your chiles on a pan.
  2. When they are golden and warm, you are going to put them in a bag for 30 minutes (this will make it easier to remove the skin because with the skin the flour will not stick).
  3. Now we remove the skin.
  4. Then you want to cut the chilli a littlle to add the cheese.
  5. Then you are going to add them to the flour.
  6. At this point you will want to beat the whites of your eggs.
  7. Once it has foamed, add one yolk at a time and beat between each yolk you add.
  8. Then you are going to grind the tomato with the onion and water until it covers the tomatoes.
  9. Once you have ground the tomatoes, you are going to add them to a pot and you will have to have the chopped squash and add it to the same and a little salt (salt to taste).
  10. Meanwhile you are going to submerge a chili in the egg and cook it in a pan with a little oil (don't lose sight of them because it will start to brown very quickly.
  11. And now you let them cook in the pot for 20 minutes. Bon Apetite!
